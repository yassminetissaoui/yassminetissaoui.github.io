Yassmine Tissaoui is interested in the place of diversity within stylized illustrative work, which she uses to explore concepts of unconventional beauty.

Having completed her associate's degree in graphic design and multimedia, she has gained clarity and understanding of the design industry and seeks to utilize her broad skill set to raise awareness on important social and ecological issues. She believes it's important to use her understanding of marketing to raise digital awareness and use her art to make the world a nicer place.

She's developed quite an interest in motion design and animation and is seeking an internship where she could learn, observe, and apply the experience she has gained
Her outside interests include reading, crocheting, cross-stitching and yoga. She's always been fond of horror and mystery literature and always seeks out diverse, unique authors. She's also a life-long gamer.
